A little explanation about turtleneck

A turtleneck is a type of high-collared top that covers part or all of the
neck. Turtlenecks are usually made of knit or wool, and are often worn
for warmth in cold weather. Their simple yet elegant design makes
them a popular choice in the fashion world, for both casual and formal
looks.
